VideoRay is pleased to introduce the M5 series thruster motor. This thruster features oil compensation for a 2,000 m (6,560 ft) depth rating, direct drive brushless motor with the motor controller built into the housing. It delivers a high thrust to input power ratio and is very efficient at low inputs. It has a low starting RPM, reverses instantly and does not cog. It can be operated with either a left or a right pitch propeller. Addressable firmware allows multiple thrusters to be connected to the same bus using a stackable plug.
